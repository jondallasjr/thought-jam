Philosophy for Everyone

Philosophy has been locked away in ivory towers for too long, at a great disservice to the world. We make philosophical discourse accessible, engaging, relevant, and fun. No small talk. Just deep conversations that matter.

We use the power of facilitated intercultural dialogue to combat sociopolitical polarization and heal societal divides. By exploring the nuanced science, theory, and emotion of today's most divisive issues from all perspectives, we create more understanding and compassionate communities.

Overcoming Cultural Barriers

In a time of intense political and social division, it's easy to assume that those who disagree with us are either uneducated or evil. By engaging in civil debate over divisive contemporary issues, we grant ourselves the opportunity to once again recognize the humanity in our opposition.
