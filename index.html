Our Mission

We use the power of facilitated and contextualized intercultural dialogue to combat sociopolitical polarization and heal societal divides. Through fun, interactive, multimedia events, we explore the nuanced science, theory, and emotion of today's most divisive issues from all perspectives.

The Experience

Deep Conversations

No small talk here. Our skilled facilitators ignite passionate, respectful debates where every voice is heard and valued. It's a safe space to voice controversial opinions and be genuinely heard.

Art & Creativity

From live music and spoken word to interactive installations, your senses feast on creativity that complements the mental stimulation. Think underground art gallery meets philosophical salon.

Intellectual Playground

Collaborative games flip your assumptions upside down. Surprise elements keep you on your intellectual toes. Every event is designed to challenge perception and spark creative thinking.

Community

A melting pot of curious minds from all walks of life. Feel the electrifying buzz of ideas colliding and evolving. Many attendees forge deep friendships through these shared intellectual adventures.
