What to Expect

01

Arrive & Connect

Step into an atmosphere where underground art gallery meets philosophical salon. Grab a themed drink, admire the artwork, and meet fellow curious minds.

02

Opening Talk

Our facilitator presents the evening's topic with an impartial, engaging lecture that explores all perspectives. No preaching, just context.

03

Small Group Discussion

Break into intimate groups for guided conversations. This is where the magic happens - deep dialogue with diverse viewpoints.

04

Interactive Elements

Games, live performances, panel Q&As, or artistic expressions that complement the discussion and keep energy high.

05

Open Networking

Continue conversations over drinks and music. Many attendees leave with new friends and shifted perspectives.

06

Take It With You

Walk away with new insights, connections, and often a fundamentally different understanding of the issue.

Our Conversation Guidelines

All Ideas Welcome

Fringe, religious, taboo - we explore the full spectrum.

Share the Air

Listen as much as you speak. Your silence has value.

Learn, Don't Win

Understanding beats being right. Changing your mind is growth.

Curiosity Over Judgment

Ask questions, don't make assumptions.

Disagree Respectfully

Challenge ideas, not people. Use "I" statements.

Assume Good Intent

Engage with the best interpretation of others' views.
