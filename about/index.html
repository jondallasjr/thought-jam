About Thought Jam

From a small book club in Hanoi to a global movement bridging divides through bold dialogue.

Our Mission

We use the power of facilitated intercultural dialogue to combat sociopolitical polarization and heal societal divides. By exploring the nuanced science, theory, and emotion of today's most divisive issues from all perspectives, we create more understanding and compassionate communities.

Our Vision

We envision a world where respectful and productive dialogue is the norm, and where all voices are heard and valued. A safe and inclusive space where people explore complex issues in entertaining ways, fostering understanding and empathy across all backgrounds and beliefs.

Our Journey

What started as a philosophy book club in the back room of a Hanoi cafe on March 3, 2017, has evolved into a global phenomenon. We began exploring the writings of Plato, Nietzsche, and Lao Tzu with a handful of curious minds. Today, we tackle the most divisive contemporary issues with thousands of engaged participants worldwide.

2017

The Beginning

Started as Hanoi Philosophy Forum, a free volunteer-run book club focused on conventional philosophy.

2018

Evolution

Introduced structured lectures and small-group discussions to make philosophy more accessible and engaging.

2019

Expansion

Shifted focus to divisive contemporary issues, adding multimedia elements, live music, and interactive games.

2020+

Global Reach

Expanded across continents and online, becoming Thought Jam. Now hosting events in 11 cities worldwide.

Core Values

01

Non-Judgment

We create safe spaces for free expression of ideas, allowing exploration of diverse thoughts without fear of ridicule or discrimination.

02

Curiosity

We nurture a spirit of inquiry and continuous learning, fostering an environment where intellectual growth flourishes.

03

Intellectual Humility

We encourage open-mindedness, recognizing our own limitations and striving to understand others' viewpoints.

04

Open Dialogue

We champion respectful communication to bridge divides, viewing conflict as an opportunity for deeper understanding.

05

Community Building

We foster environments where diverse individuals connect through meaningful dialogue, transforming strangers into collaborators.

06

Accessibility

We make complex philosophical ideas relevant and understandable, empowering individuals to engage deeply with the world.

What Makes Us Different

Impartial Exploration

We present all sides of divisive issues fairly and objectively, without pushing any agenda. Every perspective deserves serious consideration.

Entertainment First

We're not just the best philosophy event in town - we aim to be the most entertaining weekly event, period. Learning should be thrilling.

Multimedia Experience

Our events fuse rigorous discourse with live music, interactive games, commissioned artwork, and sensory elements for full immersion.

Real Community

We're not just hosting events - we're building a movement of curious minds committed to understanding over ideology.
