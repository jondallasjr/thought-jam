Our Evolution

March 2017: The Book Club

We started as the Hanoi Philosophy Forum, a simple book club tucked away in the back room of a cafe. Guests attended for free, and everyone was a volunteer. We focused exclusively on exploring conventional academic philosophers.

Incorporating Lectures

We quickly recognized that most guests arrived without completing readings, hopelessly confused. So we asked volunteers to prepare accessible presentations breaking down complex philosophical concepts. Guests loved the accessibility and the opportunity to ask questions.

Growing Beyond

Within weeks, we outgrew our back room and moved upstairs. By July 2017, we needed even more space and relocated to Bluebirds' Nest, gaining new technology and the ability to facilitate better small-group discussions.

Today: Thought Jam

We've evolved from a philosophy book club into an immersive experience combining rigorous discourse with live music, interactive games, and commissioned artwork. We're no longer just studying philosophyâ€”we're living it, creating it, and making it accessible to everyone.
